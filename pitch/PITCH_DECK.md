## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

# $9.3 BILLION

**Lost every year to remittance fees in Latin America.**

- Average fee to send money to Mexico: **5.8%**
- To Central America: **7–9%**
- To rural communities without bank access: **up to 12%**

The people who can least afford it pay the most.

A construction worker in Houston sends $500 home. His mother in Oaxaca receives $460. The other $40 bought nothing. Built nothing. Fed no one.

**$40 x 52 weeks = $2,080/year stolen from a single family.**

That is a semester of school. A year of medicine. A new roof.

## SLIDE 4 — THE SOLUTION

# Puente

A Stellar-powered remittance platform that cuts fees from 6–9% to **under 1%.**

**How it works — in three taps:**

```
Carlos (Houston)          Stellar Network          Maria (Oaxaca)
    $500 USD ──────> USDC on Stellar ──────> MXN pesos
    Fee: $2.50           ~4 seconds           Cash or digital
    (0.5%)              (not 3 days)          (her choice)
```

No bank account required on either end.
No Western Union line.
No mystery fees.

## SLIDE 5 — THE MAGIC: ON-CHAIN TANDAS

But we did not stop at remittances.

**Tandas** — the rotating savings circles that have powered Mexican communities for generations — are now on-chain.

- Maria and 9 neighbors each contribute $50/month
- Smart contract manages the rotation automatically
- Transparent. Trustless. No one can skip their turn.
- Builds on-chain credit history for people with **zero** banking history

**We are not replacing culture. We are giving culture better tools.**
